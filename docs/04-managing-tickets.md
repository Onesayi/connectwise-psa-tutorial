# Lesson 4: Managing Tickets

**Goal:** Create, assign, and close a service ticket in ConnectWise PSA.

## Steps

1. From the main menu, click **Service Desk**.

   ![Service Desk menu](../assets/screenshots/04-managing-tickets/01-service-desk-menu.png)

2. Click **New Ticket** and fill in the company, contact, and summary fields.

   ![New ticket form](../assets/screenshots/04-managing-tickets/02-new-ticket-form.png)

3. Set the ticket's status, priority, and assigned resource, then save.

4. Add notes or time entries as you work the ticket, then change the status to **Closed** when resolved.

   ![Closing a ticket](../assets/screenshots/04-managing-tickets/03-close-ticket.png)
