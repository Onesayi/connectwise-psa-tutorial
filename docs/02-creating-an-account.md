# Lesson 2: Creating an Account

**Goal:** Sign up for ConnectWise PSA (or request access through your organization) and log in for the first time.

## Steps

1. Go to the ConnectWise sign-up or demo request page and start the registration process.

   ![Sign up page](../assets/screenshots/02-creating-an-account/01-signup.png)

2. Enter your organization and contact details, then submit the request.

3. Check your email for the activation or welcome message and follow the link to set your password.

   > Tip: never store real passwords or activation links in screenshots. Redact them before adding images to the repo.

4. Log in with your new credentials and confirm you land on the main dashboard.

   ![First login](../assets/screenshots/02-creating-an-account/02-first-login.png)
