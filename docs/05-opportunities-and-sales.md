# Lesson 5: Opportunities & Sales

**Goal:** Track a potential deal from opportunity through to a sales order.

## Steps

1. From the main menu, open the **Sales** module and select **Opportunities**.

   ![Opportunities list](../assets/screenshots/05-opportunities-and-sales/01-opportunities-list.png)

2. Click **New Opportunity**, link it to a company and contact, and set the expected value and close date.

   ![New opportunity](../assets/screenshots/05-opportunities-and-sales/02-new-opportunity.png)

3. Move the opportunity through your sales stages as it progresses.

4. When the deal is won, convert the opportunity into a sales order or project.
